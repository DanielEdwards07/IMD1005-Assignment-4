## Website Description
Our project is a modern steakhouse website designed to give customers a clean and professional online experience.

The purpose of the site is to help users:
- explore the restaurant and its atmosphere
- browse the menu
- make a reservation
- learn about the restaurant
- apply for jobs through the careers page

The website is made for restaurant customers, potential guests, and job applicants.

---

## Target Users
The main target users are:
- customers looking for a place to dine
- people who want to browse food and menu options
- users who want to reserve a table
- job seekers interested in working at the restaurant

---

## Navigation Guide
The website includes 5 pages:

1. **Home**  
   Main landing page with hero section and quick links.

2. **Menu**  
   Shows the restaurant menu and food items.

3. **About Us**  
   Explains who the restaurant is, what it offers, and the overall dining experience.

4. **Reserve**  
   Lets users submit a reservation form.

5. **Careers**  
   Lets users learn about working at the restaurant and apply using the careers form.

## Design Document Summary
Our design direction was to create a steakhouse website that feels:
- warm
- modern
- premium
- inviting

We used:
- dark backgrounds
- warm accent colors
- large section cards
- strong typography
- image-based content to make the restaurant feel more real

The site was designed to feel clean and organized while still looking polished.
